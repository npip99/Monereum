Send ETH coins anonymously

Untraceable

Everytime you spend MNR, the source is obscured amongst dozens of other transactions, so that the receiver will never be able to discover who you are. Signatures will allow you to prove you're the one who sent the funds, without giving up your public address.

Everytime you receive MNR, you can generate a new receiving address and get paid without ever disclosing your identity. All of your receiving funds will seamlessly combine into the same wallet.

Each transaction you make from your wallet will only cause your funds to mix and churn even more. You can even continuously exchange money with yourself to create an impossibly complex web of transactions that, from the outside, will look like there were thousands of different people exchanging MNR between each other.

Convert to Ethereum Instantly

Use the market to generate an new anonymous Ethereum wallet from your MNR, and spend it on other Ethereum-based platforms using ETH coins that came from thin air.

This provides a service that is much easier to use than mixers or inter-coin transfers between ETH and XMR. No more trusting centralized exchanges to mix your ETH with others, or trusting them to give you XMR after you give them your ETH. Playing mixers with BTC can even be illegal if you receive a tainted coin. A MNR coin will never be tainted, as both parties will always be obscured, and you can always self-churn to disconnect yourself from the original coin.

Smart Contract Compatible

This is an implementation of the Monero protocol within the Smart Contract system of Ethereum. Anyone will be able to build Smart Contracts that exchange with MNR to obscure their transaction history - opening new opportunities for those who want to build anonymous Smart Contracts, and feeding even more transactions to churn the web.

The market found on this website is an example of what could be possible. Each trade that this market commits will have its sender and receiver obscured. MNR can be used as any Smart Contract's base coin, taking a publically disclosing Smart Contract and giving it anonymity and privacy in each transaction.
